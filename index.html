Designed as a visual lineage, the Luminous Azure Caftan collection features matching silhouettes tailored for the matriarch and the daughter. Utilizing hand-loomed Aso-Oke woven with metallic silk threads, each piece represents the passing of cultural technical mastery from one era to the next.
